Facts About Avocado

Avocados have royal roots. The podcast Gastropod reports that avocados were first used as tributes from townsfolk to local royalty in Mesoamerica.

California produces 90% of the U.S. avocado crop.

In the summer of 2017, more than 3,000,000 photos of avocado toast were uploaded to Instagram every day.
